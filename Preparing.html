    Preparing for DP Design Technology

    DP Design Technology is an exciting and rewarding experience. It is important to be prepared, and to have an understanding of the how we approach Design at the DP level.

    To best prepare, complete these three before the course begins.

    1. DO : Skills

    3D modeling and using CAD software like Fusion 360 will be an essential tool for success in DP Design Technology.

    To prepare yourself for success it is important to develop your Fusion 360 CAD modeling skills.

    Choose one of these courses to complete:

    1. Fusion 360 Express Course tutorials: If you are new to Fusion 360, or need a refresher, do this course. (Don't do this if you completed this set of tutorials in Grade 10 Product Design).
    2. Fusion 360 Intermediate Course tutorials: Complete this course if completed #1 in Grade 10.

    Each tutorial takes takes 10-15 minutes, and introduces you to a set of skills that you will find useful in the development of your design ideas.

    Steps :
    1. Watch each of the tutorial videos
    2. Create a rendered image of the final design
    3. Take a screenshot of the final product
    4. Paste it into your Fusion 360 Portfolio (This is in the Google Classroom)



    • Need help with sketching in Fusion 360: Check out these tutorials
    2. EXPLORE : Case Studies in Design

    It is important to be aware how design shapes, influences, and responds to the world around us.

    Explore one of the case studies below to learn how design is investigated and how it connects to other content areas.

    Steps :
    1. Choose a case study that is of interest to you
    2. Read the case study and review the questions. Use the links to DP Course Content to explore the content
    3. Complete the CASE Study Reflection worksheet (This is in the Google Classroom)
    4. Present your summary to the Class (we will do this in the first 2 weeks of class)
